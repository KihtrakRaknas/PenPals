Hi, We’re Pen P.A.L.S Fundraising

A place where you can not only give handmade gifts to your friends and family, but also give the gift of free education to children worldwide.

Pen P.A.L.S stands for Partnering in Advancing Literary Skills, and is a non-profit organization devoted to promoting international relationships between children and providing free literary education around the world to children who don’t receive it as easily.

This site and branch of the organization, however, sells greeting and holiday cards year round, which can easily be personalized for any occasion, gender, or person. 100% of profits are donated to Pen P.A.L.S. to pay for shipping books, purchasing school supplies to give to children worldwide.
