Get Involved

You Can Make a Difference

Write your own story!

This is the central idea of our charity! So grab a handful of papers and pencils, and use your imagination to come up with an amazing tale. Then email us, and we can discuss how you can get your story to another child who needs it and make a difference. However, this option is only open to children and teens up to the age of 17.

Get yourself a little homemade and personalized birthday card or gift-wrapping

Through this website or our Instagram fundraising account @penpals.fundraising, to order custom made cards and custom gift-wrapped presents, as well as check out our example pieces. All money raised in sales goes directly to our charity.

Donate some books!

If you're a little older than the age of 17, writing your own story may be a little out of the parameters for the charity, since our goal is to connect children worldwide. But if you've got some old books, whether they be unused workbooks, regular novels, or anything else, you can always donate them as extra supplies. Contact us to learn more.

Donate some school supplies

Along with shipping books, we also ship school supplies, including paperclips, regular/colored pencils, papers, and other supplies to children worldwide. We can always use some extra supplies, so if you have any unused supplies at the end of the year, we would greatly appreciate it.

Donate a little spare change

So we've got some books, but sometimes, we need a little bit of change to cover the costs of international shipping or buy supplies to give to schools. If donating or writing books isn't your cup of tea, we could always use a bit of cash.
